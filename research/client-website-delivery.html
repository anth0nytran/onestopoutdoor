Your Part

How You Keep the Momentum Going

We built the engine. Here's how you fuel it. These are the things only you can do — and they make the difference between a website that sits there and one that generates calls.

Post on Google Business 1-2x Per Week

This is the single highest-impact thing you can do. Snap a photo at a job site, write 2-3 sentences about what you did, mention the city, and include your phone number. That's it. Google rewards businesses that post regularly on their profile — and most of your competitors don't. It takes 2 minutes and it directly improves your local ranking.

Ask for a Google Review After Every Job

You have a perfect 5.0 rating — the quality is there. You just need volume. We'll set up a simple review link you can text to customers after a job. The goal is 5-8 new reviews per month. At that rate, you pass your direct Richmond competitor in 2 months and reach 100+ reviews in under a year.

Respond to Every Review

When someone leaves a review, reply within a day or two. A simple "Thank you [Name], great working on your patio in [city]!" is enough. Google tracks whether businesses respond, and customers read the responses.

Send Us Project Photos

Every time you finish a project, text or email us a few photos with the city and what you did. We use these to update your gallery, write blog posts, and keep your site looking fresh. Before, during, and after shots are best. Phone photos are fine — real beats polished.

Answer Leads Fast

When a quote request comes in, you get an email with everything you need — name, phone, service, timeline, address. Call them back within an hour if you can. Your competitors are slow at this. You can win business just by being the first one to pick up the phone.
