What to do every week and month to keep climbing the rankings

The 3 Things That Move the Needle Most

Ranking on Google Maps comes down to three factors: relevance (do you match the search?), distance (are you near the searcher?), and prominence (does Google trust you?). We fixed relevance with categories and description. Distance is your geography. Here's how you build prominence:

  1. Reviews — volume, velocity, and responses
  2. Google Posts — regular content proves you're active
  3. Photos — fresh project photos with proper names
