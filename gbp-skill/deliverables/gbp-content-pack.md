Insulated laminated patio covers that block heat and rain completely. Features optional skylight systems for natural light and ceiling fan installation. Energy-efficient design keeps your outdoor space comfortable year-round in the Texas heat.

**Aluminum Patio Cover**
Durable, low-maintenance aluminum patio covers with optional skylights. Resistant to rust, rot, and termites — built for Houston's humid climate. Custom sizing and color matching to blend with your existing roof and trim.
